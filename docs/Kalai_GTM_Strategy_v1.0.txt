2. Launch Strategy — Architects First
Kalai launches in a single vertical: architects and interior designers in South India (Chennai, Bangalore, Hyderabad). This is not a limitation — it is a deliberate moat-building strategy. Suganth is an architect. D2V is the first case study. The community is small enough to own completely and large enough to generate word-of-mouth.

2.1 Pre-Launch (Before V1 Ships)
Talk to 10 architects and interior designers personally. Use the 5-question interview script. Refine onboarding based on their language.
Build a waitlist at kalai.io with a simple landing page. Goal: 200 signups before launch.
Document D2V's Kalai journey publicly: 'We're building a marketing tool and we're our own beta user.' Post weekly on LinkedIn and Instagram.
Join and contribute to architecture communities: CoA forums, ArchDaily India communities, design-focused Facebook groups.
Apply for Meta Advanced Access for Instagram DM API on Day 1.
